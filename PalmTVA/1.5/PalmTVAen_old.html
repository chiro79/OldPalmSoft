PalmTVA 1.2 (French version) PalmOS (3.x - 4.x - 5.x)

This program allows the calculate the tax from a specified amount. 5.5%  and 19.6% buttons are present. Custom tip (from 1% to 65%) is also allowed. Typing the total allows also to get the amount before the tip.

This program is FREE.
